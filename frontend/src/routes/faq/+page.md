# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is PriceHound?

PriceHound is an **unofficial** pricing calculator designed to help you estimate costs for Datadog services. It provides a simple interface to:

- Browse all available Datadog products and their pricing tiers
- Build custom quotes with multiple products and quantities
- Compare annual vs. monthly vs. on-demand pricing
- Share quotes via public URLs
- Export quotes as JSON or printable PDFs

PriceHound aims to make Datadog pricing more transparent and easier to understand, helping teams budget and plan their observability infrastructure.

## What PriceHound is NOT

PriceHound is **not**:

- **An official Datadog tool** — This is a community project, not endorsed or maintained by Datadog, Inc.
- **A billing or payment system** — You cannot purchase Datadog services through PriceHound.
- **A guarantee of pricing** — Prices are scraped from public Datadog documentation and may not reflect negotiated enterprise rates, promotions, or recent changes.
- **A replacement for Datadog sales** — For accurate quotes, especially for enterprise needs, contact Datadog directly.

Always verify pricing with Datadog before making purchasing decisions.

## Is PriceHound affiliated with Datadog?

**No.** PriceHound is an independent, open-source project with no affiliation to Datadog, Inc.

- We do not have access to Datadog's internal pricing systems
- We are not authorized to sell or resell Datadog services
- Datadog trademarks and product names belong to Datadog, Inc.

PriceHound simply aggregates publicly available pricing information from <a href="https://www.datadoghq.com/pricing/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">datadoghq.com/pricing</a> to provide a convenient estimation tool.

## How accurate is the pricing data?

PriceHound scrapes pricing data directly from Datadog's public pricing pages. While we strive to keep data current:

- Prices may lag behind official updates
- Some products or pricing tiers may be missing
- Enterprise or volume discounts are not reflected
- Regional pricing variations may not be captured

Use PriceHound for **estimation purposes only**. For binding quotes, contact Datadog sales.

## Can I save and share my quotes?

Yes! PriceHound allows you to:

- **Save quotes** with a shareable public URL
- **Password-protect** quotes for privacy
- **Clone and edit** existing quotes
- **Export** quotes as JSON for backup or sharing
- **Print** quotes as PDF documents

Quotes are stored for a limited time. For long-term storage, export your quotes as JSON.

## Is my data secure?

- Quotes are stored with randomly generated IDs
- Optional password protection encrypts access
- No personal information is collected
- No analytics or tracking beyond basic server logs

PriceHound is designed with privacy in mind. However, as an open-source project, use at your own discretion for sensitive pricing information.
